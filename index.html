Emergency & Safety Guidelines

Critical procedural knowledge to protect yourself and your family during an alert

Security Threats

Active Banditry or Security Intrusion

  • Identify clean egress points. Maintain absolute silence and lock down internal entries.
  • Mute all cellular devices and extinguish house lighting arrays immediately.
  • File a secure silent notification using the SafeAlert platform once safe.
Fire Hazard

Building and Residential Fires

  • Evacuate the structure immediately. Avoid using mechanical elevations or lifts.
  • Crawl beneath thick smoke arrays to preserve clean oxygen routes.
  • Dial 112 instantly or contact the Federal Fire Service Sani Abacha Road hub.
Flood Threat

Flooding and Severe Weather

  • Seek robust elevated landscapes. Avoid navigating active running water routes.
  • Disconnect master electrical circuit boxes to eliminate active ground shocks.
  • Coordinate with ZASEMA and NEMA teams during flash events.
